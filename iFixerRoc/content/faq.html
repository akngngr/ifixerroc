Frequently Asked Questions

How do iFixerROC's appointments work?

We repair devices based on customer appointments. After you complete the appointment form, our team will contact you via email and confirm your appointment dates.

How long is the warranty on repaired devices?

We offer 30 day limited malfunctioning warranty on all repairs from the day your device was repaired.

Where is iFixerROC located?

We do not have a specific location or a store, although we only service in the Rochester area based on your appointments.

How can I contact iFixerROC

You can contact us via phone or email. Contact information can be found in the About Us page.

My device is not listed in the device repairs.

You can contact us with your device information and we will provide you a quote for your device repair.
